Rae Burket Shoes expands to 5 new locations around the globe

  • Posted on
    April 28, 2015
  • Tags
    Rae Burket  |   Location
  • Comments
    8

Rae Burket Shoes is currently sold by retailers throughout the United States and nearly 30 countries abroad; the company also operates a series of 'Rae Burket Boutique stores in the United States' and have been donated worldwide.

Rae Burket Shoes is proud to announce its expansion of its boutique stores in the global market, capitalizing on high fashion industry countries, such as Italy, France, Hong Kong, Spain, and Germany. Look for the next blog post to view the specific locations of these new branches.
